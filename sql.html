Data Analysis

Average Aircraft statistics according to their mode of propulsion

SQL and Tableau were used to clean the data and visualise findings respectively,
in order to show the difference between aircraft according to their engine type.
SQL code and the dataset used for this project can be found here

The following table shows the total number of aircraft engine types analysed, and their respective percentage.
Followed by a graphic proportionally representing the below data.
The most common type of propultion was piston, being almost 4x as numerous as Jet engines.

The graph below shows airplane performance statistics.
Piston aircraft have the lowest performance and the least amount of range.
Turboprop and Jetprop aircraft dynamically are very similar, with Jetprops being more capable.
Propjets have higher performance and range, however their maxspeed is similar to Turboprops and Jetprops.
Jets are by far the most capable, their performance and range is significantly higher over other propulsion types in every category.

The final graph shows the differences between the Average Length, Width, and Weight of the airplanes.
Length and Width are in metres, whilst Weight is shown in metric tonnes.
Whilst Piston, Turboprop, and Jetprop driven aircraft have similar weight the Piston airplanes are the smallest and lightest.
Propjets are about 2.5x the weight of the other planes, but are only 3-5 meters longer.
Jets on the other hand are the heaviest planes having over 3x the weight of an average Turboprop plane.
In addition they are the largest planes being over twice the size of a piston driven aircraft.

Conclusions based on the analysis

1:
Piston aircraft
1. Are the lightest and smallest.
2. Have 247HP, less than half the horsepower of Turboprops and Jetprops.
3. Have the least amount of range; 646 Nautical miles.

2:
Turboprops, and Jetprops
1. Are similar in all aspects, however Jetprops all around have higher figures than Turboprops.
2. Jetprops have the second largest wingspan after Jets.

3:
Jet engined aircraft
1. Have the highest performance.
2. Are the heaviest and largest aircraft.
3. Have the most range at over 3,000 Nautical miles.

4:
As power, speed, and range increases so does the weight and size of the aircraft.
